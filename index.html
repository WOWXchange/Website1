Key Features



Airdrop of Listing Fees for Token Holders

A unique token distribution system for listing fees. ICOs pay lesser for listing with us and WOWX users get payout simply for holding our tokens

Crypto-to-Fiat Support for Merchant

WOWX will establish its own subsidiary platform that functions as a cryptocurrency payment service provider, enabling payment services to e-commerce merchants worldwide for their goods and services online and offline.

WOWX Smart Card

WOWX will be integrating a hardware technology that functions as a portable, secure storage device, which may be furthered to include a cryptocurrency-spending function. Device comes with multi-factor authentication.

Echo Trade

Our system allows everyday users the option to trade and invest based on real-time actions and decisions of top traders, automatically processing real-time market movements.

Get More With Us




Receive Cashback on Trading Fees



WOWX will commit a percentage of revenue taken from trading fees to incentivize users to invite other traders to trade on the platform. Users can invite others via a referral code unique to them and those who successfully invite others to trade on WOWX will receive a share of the invited person's trading fee as a bonus.



Grow Your Account Levels



While you earn cashbacks, WOWX also encourages you to grow your account. With different tiers of multiple benefits, users are incentivized to trade and create more activity on the platform.



Low Trading Prices



To encourage more Makers, should they choose to pay trading fees in WOWX Tokens rather than the trading currency, WOWX will give a 50% discount on trading fee for users on top of their trading account levels.
